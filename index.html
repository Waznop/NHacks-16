MOLES



Player 1: Arrow Keys + Right Shift to dig/pop

Player 2: WASD + E to dig/pop





Relive your childhood gaming moments!

Challenge a friend to a duel!

First mole to pop up under the other mole wins.



Be careful: you die if you pop up under a rock.

Also, digging uses energy.

If you have none left, you will surface!



If you enjoyed the game, share it on Facebook!
